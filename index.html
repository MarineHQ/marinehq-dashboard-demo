The dream: the crew keep doing exactly what they do now, and the dashboard fills itself in. Here's how.
1

Crew fill the End-of-Day report

Just like today — they type what they did on each boat. No dropdowns, no categories to pick, nothing new to learn.

2

The words get matched to the Job Menu

"Full wash down" is recognised as Scheduled → Wash down. "Replaced bilge pump" as Unscheduled → Plumbing. The system tags it automatically.

3

The job ticks itself DONE

It appears on that boat's board — green, with the date and crew name straight off the card. Nobody ticks anything by hand.

4

You & Jack sign off the month

You just review each boat and hit Sign off. The outstanding jobs stand out in amber — nothing slips through.
